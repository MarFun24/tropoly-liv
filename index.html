Overview

Make the content
earn what it's worth.

LIV Magazine's print product is strong. The editorial quality is there. The sales process works. What is missing is the infrastructure that turns all of that into compounding digital value, a website that ranks, an email list that converts, and a distribution pipeline that does not depend on the ownership team manually posting to every platform.

Three projects address the highest-leverage gaps. Together, they build the digital foundation that protects and amplifies the print business, without disrupting the editorial and production workflow already in place.

Project 01

Content Distribution Automation

A single-trigger workflow that takes a finished story from Dropbox and automatically distributes it to every digital channel, simultaneously, without manual effort from the editorial or ownership team.

Project 02

Website Rebuild & Digital Presence

Replace the non-functional B12 site with a true media platform, searchable, story-archived, SEO-optimized, and built to give advertisers the digital metrics they need to justify their spend.

Project 03

Email List Rebuild & Newsletter Automation

Clean and audit the FlowDesk list (estimated ~50K contacts), rebuild the segmentation strategy, and automate a newsletter format that actually converts, targeting industry-leading open rates of 40, 45%.

Looking Ahead

Phase 2: Tropoly OS

Tropoly OS, applied to LIV: ongoing workflow design, automation, and operational intelligence

Phase 1 builds The Machine and gets it running. Phase 2 is where Tropoly embeds more deeply, using the Tropoly OS framework to extend automation across the business, build data visibility into operations, and develop custom tools that turn LIV's digital infrastructure into a genuine competitive advantage.

Tropoly OS engagements are scoped around specific workflow and systems problems, not open-ended retainers. Each project is defined, built, and handed over with documentation, so the team owns what gets built.

01

Advertiser reporting & data visibility

Automated reporting infrastructure that pulls story reach, email engagement, and website traffic into a clean dashboard, then delivers it to advertisers on a schedule. The sales team gains real numbers to sell with. Advertisers get proof of performance without anyone having to pull a report manually.

02

Operational dashboards & KPI frameworks

A real-time view of what matters: subscriber growth, open rates, distribution performance by channel, sales pipeline activity, and ICONIC series production status. Built once, updated automatically, accessible without technical knowledge.

Extend automation into the parts of the business Phase 1 does not touch: sales agent pipeline tracking, ICONIC series production and fulfilment, regional edition coordination, and any new platforms or distribution channels LIV moves into. Each workflow is scoped, built, and handed over.

Scope note: Phase 2 is not included in this proposal. It is offered as a signal of what Tropoly OS can do once the foundation is in place. If there are specific workflow problems worth solving now, we are happy to discuss scoping them into Phase 1 or as a parallel workstream.
